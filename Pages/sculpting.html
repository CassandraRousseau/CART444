Eagle Arm (2018)

This project is about discovering working with wood material. It is an eagle-inspired scultired sculpture in which the wearer can move the wing by pulling the cord attached to the project.

Made with wood, acrylic, cords, fake feather and fake snow.

Esprits du marais (2019)

Composition of plaster sculptures representing spirits of a swamp. This project is based on the exploration of the material. Pigments were mixed with the plaster to accentuate the vitality of the characters.

Made with plaster, pigments, buttons and Christmas canes. Dimensions: 35,56 x 50,8 x 27,94cm.
