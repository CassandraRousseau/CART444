Chemin des pigments (2019)

Creation of a model for a public art project.The location used for this model would be in the cafeteria of the CÉGEP du Vieux-Montréal. I represented the dynamism of the place in abstract form.
Made with acrylic on wood.
Dimension: 45.72 x 60.96cm.

Implosion/Explosion (2019)

Creation of a diptych on the theme of transfiguration. I wanted to tackle the subject of the mutation of emotions. I worked on texture using acrylics and gel, as well as marouflaged texts.
Made with acrylic on wood.
Dimensions: 2 x (35.56 x 30.48cm).

Good Girl (2019)

Painting on the theme of child abuse. I created a gloomy, unhealthy atmosphere to express the theme. I sought a rich and varied color palette for this project.

Made with acrylic on canvas. Dimension: 91,44x 68,58cm.

Tristesse cachée (2020)

An abstract painting in which I wanted to work with texture and a rich, varied color palette. This painting exudes a morose, sad mood with its cold nuances.

Made with acrylic on canvas. Dimension: 50.8 x 40.64cm.
